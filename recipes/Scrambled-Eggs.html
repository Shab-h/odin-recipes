Scrambled Eggs Recipes

Descriptions

This scramble eggs recipes will take an approximately 6 min total. With a 2 min prep time and 4 min cook, it should yeild 1 serving.

The following recipes is also been reccomended by the top michielan star cheffs.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 teaspoon mayonnaise or salad dressing
  • 1 teaspoon water (Optional)
  • 1 teaspoon margarine or butter
  • 1 pinch slat and pepper for taste

Steps

  1. Whisk together the eggs in a cup or small bowl
  2. Add mayonnaise and water and continue to stir with a fork
  3. Turn on the pan and wait till its hot
  4. Then pour in the eggs, and stir constantly as they cook
  5. Make sure to fold the eggs gently on it self after it starts to firm up a bit
  6. Remove the eggs to a plate when they are set, but still moist (Do not over cook)
  7. Add salt and papper (Mkae sure not to add before eggs are on the plate)
